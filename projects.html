The first entry I'm going to put on this list is this website. It is entirely coded by me in HTML. I used to have a Squarespace website that looked very similar, because I am designing this based off that. I had the Squarespace website for 1 year, and started designing this about a month before it expired, as I didn't want to pay for it anymore. I started to learn how to code HTML on Codecademy, with lots of help from Stack Overflow and W3Schools. It didn't take that long for me to move to VSCode for a better workflow. As of writing this, it has already been a very fun journey creating this website. I loved the idea of having a place to show off my ideas and projects, while also having a full portfolio of my online creations.
